The Game

CS-001 is a (currently unnamed) game about Oliver Robineaux and a creepy gas station. Delayed by a traffic jam, he drives into the night until running low on fuel and being forced to fill up at a strange unbranded store with signs reading only "GAS." The game is in a top-down horror/narrative style and will be available whenever I finish it. If you play it and have some feedback, feel free to get in contact and let me know!

This is my first-ever "real" game where I've done the code, art, sound design; everything. See below for links to the latest builds and, later, a few screenshots.
