"HENRY"

A STORY BY MXBLAH

The Story

"HENRY" is a story about Nathan Pasternack, a recent college graduate desperately searching for literally any job other than food service or retail. But when he accidentally boards the wrong bus, he's taken to a sort of twisted reflection of an office building that can't be easily escaped. He'll need to be clever and determined to root out the secrets of this place, and to make it out in one piece.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and was run over Discord on the Clockworksun Stories Discord server. The story was completed on January 26th, 2022, and will no longer be updating.
