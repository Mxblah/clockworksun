REFLECTIVE SILVER LOVE AND JUSTICE!

A STORY BY MXBLAH

The Story

Reflective Silver Love and Justice! is a story about Brianna Whitman (aka Silver) and a whole bunch of other heroes as they learn about their powers, the angels and demons that grant them, and what exactly these celestials actually want. Do the angels truly have everyone's best interests at heart? Are the demons really all that bad? Can heroes of light and dark... get along? But before that, they've got to figure out how to balance a normal life with the responsibilities of a hero, and how to keep their own secrets safe from those who would exploit them.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and is run over Discord on the Clockworksun Stories Discord server.
