END OF EVERYTHING

A STORY BY MXBLAH

The Story

End of Everything is a story about Elen, Natira, and Kirin, possibly the last three people alive after the end of the universe. Stuck on one tiny entropy-defying spaceship, they must keep it operating long enough to... do what, exactly? Nothing is left in the universe. Why are they bothering? Is there a reason to keep going at all? Maybe. If there is, they're going to find it.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and was run over Discord on the Clockworksun Stories Discord server. The story was completed on August 5th, 2021, and will no longer be updating.
