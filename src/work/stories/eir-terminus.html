EIR TERMINUS

A STORY BY MXBLAH

The Story

Eir Terminus is a story about Ellie Lynran, a bounty hunter; Mel Alborn, her handler and girlfriend; and Qaniit, a native of a low-tech planet recently introduced into the galactic community. When someone frames Ellie and Mel for crimes they (surprisingly) didn't commit, and Qaniit begins to uncover a strange corporate conspiracy, they'll have to work together to piece things together before it's too late.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and is run over Discord on the Clockworksun Stories Discord server.
