THE MAN IN THE MAROON SUIT

A STORY BY MXBLAH

The Story

The Man in the Maroon Suit is a story about Suyeon Che, more commonly known as Su, an experimental propulsion scientist at Korman Aerospace Engineering. When Su starts seeing a terrifying man she calls the Devil in her dreams (and, worse, when he starts appearing in real life as well), she has to get to the bottom of the mystery before he makes good on his promise to kill her. A city destroyed by fire, a cult worshipping the moon, and a strange symbol burned into Su's hand: they have to be connected somehow. There's not much time to find out.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and is run over Discord on the Clockworksun Stories Discord server.
