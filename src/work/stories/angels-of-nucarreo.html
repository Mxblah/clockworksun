ANGELS OF NUCARREO

A STORY BY MXBLAH

The Story

Angels of Nucarreo is a story about Jessamine, who has recently Ascended to become the newest Angel of the Cathedra. She will gain much in divine ability, and must give just as much to keep her city safe from the threats that only Angels can face.

But... it's not all black-and-white, is it? There's corruption in the Cathedra, secrets buried deeper than most dare to tread, and a dangerous shortage of Angels to deal with it all. Soon, events will come to a head and something will break. When that will be, what that will be, and whether anyone can put it back together... those are all questions Jessamine will have to answer. Or face the consequences for her ignorance.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and is run over Discord on the Clockworksun Stories Discord server.
