GLORBULON

A STORY BY MRBLAH

The Story

Glorbulon is a story about Jess, a courier for Lockheed-Bolte Incorporated. While on an evening out with her android friend Nine, the two learn about a series of mysterious disappearances shocking the galaxy. Several high-profile starliners, and even one isolated planet, have vanished completely! Never one to turn down a poor idea (at least while tipsy), Jess agrees to help Nine track down the source of these disappearances.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and was run over Discord on the MTU Huskies Discord server. The story was completed on June 16th, 2020, and will no longer be updating.
