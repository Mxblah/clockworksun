CITY OF GLASS

A STORY BY MXBLAH

The Story

City of Glass is a story about the titular City of Glass: the place where the dregs of the universe end up. Vivienne Redmarsh has traveled there along with her mentor Arthur in search of the long-forgotten secrets that rot amidst the City's depths. But the trip takes a severe turn for the worse when Arthur goes missing and Vivienne discovers she is a Diver: one of the rare few people who can physically descend below the waves to hunt for treasure and ancient knowledge. Now, using her new abilities to seek out the truth while trying to stay on the right side of dangers both above and below, Vivienne must find Arthur and confront the fear lurking within her own bloodline if she is to have any hope of leaving the City alive.

The story is written in a choose-your-own adventure format and is run over Discord on the Clockworksun Stories Discord server.
